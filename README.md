# 📌 Task 3 – Customer Churn Analysis

## Problem Statement

Customer churn is one of the most important business challenges faced by subscription-based companies.

The goal of this project was to identify patterns and factors that influence customer churn using exploratory data analysis techniques.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Name:**
WA_Fn-UseC_-Telco-Customer-Churn

**Dataset Size:**
7043 customer records

**Features:**
- Customer demographics
- Subscription details
- Internet services
- Contract information
- Payment methods
- Monthly charges
- Total charges
- Churn status

---

## Data Cleaning Process

Several preprocessing steps were performed:

### ✔ Missing Value Handling
- Identified blank values in TotalCharges
- Converted data types appropriately
- Replaced missing values using median imputation

### ✔ Data Type Corrections
- Converted numerical columns into proper numeric formats
- Verified consistency across features

### ✔ Data Validation
- Checked for null values
- Verified dataset integrity
- Examined feature distributions

---

## Visualizations Generated

### 📈 Customer Churn Distribution

Analyzed overall churn percentage across customers.

### 📈 Contract Type vs Churn

Examined how different contract types affect customer retention.

### 📈 Payment Method vs Churn

Identified payment methods associated with higher churn.

### 📈 Monthly Charges vs Churn

Studied the relationship between monthly billing and customer attrition.

### 📈 Tenure vs Churn

Analyzed how customer lifetime influences churn probability.

### 📈 Correlation Heatmap

Explored numerical relationships between important variables.

---

## Key Insights

### 🔹 Long-Term Contracts Reduce Churn

Customers with yearly contracts showed significantly lower churn rates compared to month-to-month subscribers.

### 🔹 New Customers Are More Likely To Leave

Customers with shorter tenure exhibited higher churn behavior.

### 🔹 Higher Monthly Charges Increase Churn Risk

Customers paying higher monthly fees showed increased likelihood of cancellation.

### 🔹 Automatic Payment Methods Improve Retention

Electronic payment methods demonstrated stronger customer retention patterns.

---

## Business Impact

These findings can help businesses:

- Improve customer retention strategies
- Reduce revenue loss
- Optimize subscription plans
- Enhance customer engagement programs

# 🎬 Task 4 – Netflix Dataset Analysis

## Problem Statement

Streaming platforms generate massive amounts of content data.

The objective of this project was to explore Netflix's content catalog and uncover trends related to content type, release years, ratings, genres, and geographic distribution.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Name:**
Netflix Titles Dataset

**Dataset Size:**
Over 8,000 titles

**Features:**

- Title
- Type
- Director
- Cast
- Country
- Date Added
- Release Year
- Rating
- Duration
- Genre

---

## Data Cleaning Process

### ✔ Missing Value Analysis

Identified and managed missing values in:

- Director
- Cast
- Country
- Date Added

### ✔ Feature Formatting

Converted dates and standardized text fields for analysis.

### ✔ Dataset Preparation

Prepared data for visualization and trend analysis.

---

## Visualizations Generated

### 🎥 Content Type Distribution

Movies vs TV Shows comparison.

### 🌍 Top Countries Producing Content

Countries with highest Netflix content contributions.

### 🎭 Top Genres Analysis

Most popular genres available on Netflix.

### 📅 Release Year Distribution

Content growth across years.

### ⭐ Ratings Distribution

Audience rating classifications.

### 📈 Content Added Over Time

Netflix catalog expansion trends.

---

## Key Insights

### 🔹 Movies Dominate Netflix Catalog

Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows.

### 🔹 United States Leads Content Production

The majority of Netflix content originates from the United States.

### 🔹 Dramatic Growth After 2015

Content volume increased rapidly in recent years.

### 🔹 Diverse Genre Portfolio

Netflix offers content across multiple genres catering to varied audiences.

### 🔹 Global Expansion Strategy

Content production spans numerous countries worldwide.

---

## Business Impact

These insights can help:

- Content acquisition teams
- Streaming analysts
- Recommendation system developers
- Entertainment industry researchers

understand content trends and platform growth strategies.
